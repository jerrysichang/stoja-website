How it works

Stoja is designed around urgency, not task list perfection.

1) Capture tasks quickly

Open Stoja and drop in what needs doing. No complex hierarchy, no setup tax.

2) Feel time pressure

Stoja keeps your attention constrained so tasks move from "maybe later" to "done".

3) Review and repeat

Clear completed tasks, adjust priority, and keep momentum with minimal overhead.
